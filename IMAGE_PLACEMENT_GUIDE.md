## Design Rationale

### Why These Placements Work

**Homepage Hero (studio-atmosphere.jpg)**
- Purple lighting creates mystery and professionalism
- Matches site color scheme (coral/teal/purple accents)
- High emotional impact for first-time visitors
- Instagram-worthy aesthetic

**Studio Page Hero (studio-full-room.jpg)**
- Shows complete professional setup
- Visible acoustic treatment builds trust
- Clean, organized space appeals to serious musicians
- Door visible = welcoming, accessible

**Service Cards (keyboard images)**
- Equipment close-ups prove quality
- Wide shots help visualize space
- Authentic photos > stock images
- Builds immediate credibility

**CTA Section (multi-keyboard)**
- Shows variety and options
- Red Nord keyboard = color pop
- Multiple workstations = comprehensive service
- Encourages booking action

## Responsive Behavior

All images are implemented with:
- `bg-cover`: Crops intelligently to fit
- `bg-center`: Keeps focal point centered
- `object-fit`: Maintains aspect ratio
- Hover effects: Subtle zoom on cards
- Dark overlay: Ensures text readability

### Mobile Optimization
- Hero images: Slightly cropped on mobile
- Card images: Reduced height on small screens
- Touch-friendly: No hover effects on mobile
- Performance: Lazy loading where appropriate
